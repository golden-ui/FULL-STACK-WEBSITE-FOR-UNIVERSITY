University Centers And Their Information

Pune Center

Curriculum: Offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Computer Science, Commerce, and Management Studies.

Admission Process: Online application via university portal. Entrance test for select programs. Merit-based selection.

Nagpur Center

Curriculum: Includes programs in Engineering, Law, and Arts. Practical and project-based learning integrated.

Admission Process: Admission through state-level entrance exams and university application. Counseling conducted post shortlisting.

Visit Nagpur Center Website

Kharghar (Mumbai) Center

Curriculum: Business Administration, Finance, and Data Analytics programs with industry partnerships.

Admission Process: Direct applications accepted. Interview and academic score-based selection.
